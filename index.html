About this app

NextGenTV is a premium IPTV for Android TV, Fire TV, Smart TVs, and Android phones. Stream 1500+ Indian and international channels with ultra-low buffering and fast channel switching. Enjoy channels across all categories, including movies, sports, news, kids, and full regional coverage in Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, Malayalam, Kannada, Bengali, Marathi, and more. Features include TV Guide (EPG), multi-source streaming for stable playback, remote control support, auto-start on TV boot, and a dedicated Cricket/IPL section.
